Privacy Policy

I value your privacy and want to be transparent about how data is handled when you visit this website. This website does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, phone number, or IP address.

Use of Google Analytics

To help me understand how people interact with this website and improve the overall experience, I use Google Analytics, a third-party tool that collects general usage data. While I don’t personally collect or store any of your personal information, Google Analytics gathers anonymous data through cookies and similar technologies.

Here’s what Google Analytics may collect:

  • Country of access: This tells me the general region (by country) where visitors are coming from. It helps me understand where my readers are based and how to better serve different audiences.
  • Page title and screen class: This is part of the "Pages and Screens" report, which shows how users engage with specific pages or screens. It helps me see which posts are most popular and how people move through the site.
  • Traffic acquisition: This report shows how visitors arrive at the blog—whether through search engines, social media, direct visits, or other sources. It helps me understand which channels are most effective in reaching new readers.

How I Use This Information

The information gathered is used solely for analytical purposes, such as:

  • Monitoring site traffic and visitor behavior
  • Identifying popular content and optimizing the site layout
  • Improving the readability, speed, and overall experience of the blog
  • Understanding what kind of content resonates most with my audience

I do not use this data to identify individual users, and I do not sell or share any of this information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer. Just be aware that doing so might slightly affect how some parts of the blog function.

Your Consent

By continuing to browse and use this site, you’re giving your consent to the use of cookies and the collection of anonymous data via Google Analytics, as described above. If you’re not comfortable with that, feel free to adjust your browser settings or stop using the site.

Updates to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, analytics tools, or privacy practices. Any updates will be posted here with a new date. By continuing to use the blog after changes are made, you accept the updated policy.
